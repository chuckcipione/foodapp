A little bit about us

Parameal is a service to have fresh home-cooked food without cooking or eating out. Resturants can a toll on your body, time, and wallet while cooking/meal-prepping can become an inconvience. Parameal solves this by providing authentic home foods to promote eating well. We work as a peer-to-peer exchange, which means you can become a chef and cater to others. We help discover the inner chef in you and connect to the all the foodies out there. You can become your own boss, work any amount of hours, whenever, wherever. So whether you are looking to have meals in the way of the modern age or become a high-earning chef, we can make it happen.
